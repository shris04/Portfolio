About Me

Hi! I’m a senior at New York University studying mechanical engineering with an interest in human-centered product design. I approach engineering with attention to form, detail, and human–machine interaction, aiming to balance performance and aesthetics with intention and comfort in mind. I’m drawn to designing products that improve everyday experiences, whether through assistive technology, consumer products, or innovative physical solutions that connect with people. I believe the strongest engineering is defined not just by how well it works, but by how well it serves the people it’s designed for.
