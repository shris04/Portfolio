Iron Man MK3 Suitcase

Replicating the Iron Man MK3 suit that is able to transform into a suitcase mechanism, focusing on scale design, compression, and mechanical deployment.

Goal Design the Iron Man MK3 to scale and its suitcase.
Software SolidWorks, SolidWorks Visualize, Excel Tolerance Stack-Up Analysis.
The Problem

The primary challenge was fitting the suit into a portable suitcase design without compromising the functionality and aesthetic accuracy of the suit. The solution resulted in a modualized design where limbs are detached from the body.

Key Requirements
  • To scale of MK3 suit.
  • Iron Patriot configuration.
  • Suitcase transformation.
